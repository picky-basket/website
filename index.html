How it works

From recipe to doorstep, in three steps.

No separate trips to the butcher, the market, and the corner store. One basket, built around what you're actually cooking.

STEP 01

Choose a dish

Search a recipe like Jollof Rice or Banku & Tilapia, or browse fresh categories yourself — herbs to meat to grain.

STEP 02

We gather it fresh

Every ingredient is picked and quality-checked, from the butcher's cut to the last scotch bonnet pepper.

STEP 03

It arrives cold, ready to cook

Cold-chain delivery keeps meat and produce fresh from pickup to your door — no detour through a warm van.

Our mission

We got tired of the multi-stop grocery run — so we skipped it.

One pot of soup can mean four different stops: the butcher, the fishmonger, the vegetable stall, the spice seller. Picky Basket combines all of it into one basket, sourced fresh and delivered cold, so cooking a proper meal doesn't start with an hour of errands.
